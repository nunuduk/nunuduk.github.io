Adiabatic GeMM Accelerator 2025

4×4 systolic-array-based GeMM accelerator with Bfloat16 PEs and weight-stationary architecture. Custom adiabatic scratchpad buffer reduces active power by several orders of magnitude over conventional designs.

Full-Duplex UART Controller 2025

Designed and verified in SystemC with baud rate control and error detection — completed in 2.5 weeks. Physical design in Cadence Stratus/Genus/Innovus, exceeded all PPA targets. Taped out in TSMC 180nm.

Fan Speed Controller ASIC 2024

Fan speed controller in Verilog with temperature sensor interface and multi-speed control logic. Synthesized using the eFabless chipIgnite flow in GF180nm. Passed Multi-Project Wafer and Tapeout tests.

RNN Pick-up Line Generator 2023

GRU-based text generator in TensorFlow with ~6M parameters, trained on 10,000+ graded pick-up lines. Optimized over 225 epochs with batch norm, dropout, and L2 regularization (test loss 1.7). Paired with a classifier that scores outputs.
