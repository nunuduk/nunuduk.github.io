About

I'm an Electrical Engineering student at the University of Notre Dame, graduating May 2026. My work sits at the intersection of ultra-low-power IC design and hardware verification — specifically, I spend most of my time building UVM testbenches and designing adiabatic circuits that recover energy rather than waste it.

I've been a research assistant in Notre Dame's chip design group for the past two years, and an NDNano Undergraduate Research Fellow before that. I've also mentored several cohorts of undergrads through their first digital IC design projects.

Outside the lab, I run the design side of Strike Magazine and help out with the Chinese Cultural Society.
